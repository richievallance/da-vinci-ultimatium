## Chronological Framework

*Based on verified documentary evidence. Dates are established scholarship unless marked [HYPOTHESIS].*

### Phase 1: Vinci and Florence — Formation (1452–1482)
- **1452**: Born, Anchiano near Vinci
- **c.1466–1476**: Apprenticeship, Verrocchio's workshop, Florence
- **1472**: Enrolled in Florentine painters' guild
- **1476–1478**: *Gap years* — movements uncertain; Ottoman letters (Richter §§1336–7) suggest possible eastern travel [HYPOTHESIS under investigation in figura-ultima]
- **c.1478–1482**: Independent work in Florence; Adoration of the Magi begun (unfinished)

### Phase 2: First Milanese Period — Mechanics and Court (1482–1499)
- **c.1482**: Arrival in Milan; service to Ludovico Sforza
- **c.1482–1499**: *Madrid Codex I* composed — the master engineering treatise
  → **Repository:** [da-vinci-mechanica](https://github.com/richievallance/da-vinci-mechanica)
- **c.1489–1494**: Anatomical studies begin in Milan
- **c.1490**: Vitruvian Man drawing (Gallerie dell'Accademia, Venice)
- **c.1494–1498**: Last Supper, Santa Maria delle Grazie
- **1499**: French invasion; departure from Milan

### Phase 3: Wandering Years — Venice, Mantua, Florence (1499–1506)
- **1499–1500**: Venice, then Mantua (Isabella d'Este portrait study)
- **1500–1506**: Return to Florence; anatomical studies continue
- **c.1503–1506**: Mona Lisa begun; Battle of Anghiari commission

### Phase 4: Second Milanese Period — Hydraulics and Science (1506–1513)
- **1506–1513**: Return to Milan under French governance
- **c.1508–1511**: *Madrid Codex II* composed — universal architecture thesis
  → **Repository:** [principia-mechanica](https://github.com/richievallance/principia-mechanica) / [corpus-leonardianum](https://github.com/richievallance/corpus-leonardianum)
- **c.1510**: Major anatomical studies with Marcantonio della Torre

### Phase 5: Rome — Optics and Light (1513–1516)
- **1513–1516**: Rome, under Giuliano de' Medici
- Optical studies; mirror-making experiments; Codex Atlanticus material
  → **Repository:** [solarscorch](https://github.com/richievallance/solarscorch) (CA673 solar optics)

### Phase 6: France — Late Notebooks and Convergence (1516–1519)
- **1516**: Invited to France by Francis I; Château d'Amboise
- **1517**: Visit from Cardinal Luigi d'Aragona; three paintings shown (Last Supper panel, St John, Virgin)
- **1518**: *Feast of Saint John the Baptist* — CA673 solar alignment study [HYPOTHESIS]
  → **Repository:** [solarscorch](https://github.com/richievallance/solarscorch)
- **2 May 1519**: Death, Amboise
