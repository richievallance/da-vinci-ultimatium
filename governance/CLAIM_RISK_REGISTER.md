## Purpose

This register tracks the risk profile of every major claim — the probability that it would be challenged, and the nature of the most likely challenge. High-risk claims require additional evidential support before publication.

---

## Risk Levels

| Level | Definition |
|---|---|
| LOW | Well-supported by primary sources; unlikely to be challenged on evidential grounds |
| MEDIUM | Well-supported but depends on interpretive step(s); specialist challenge possible |
| HIGH | Significant interpretive component; likely to generate specialist debate |
| CRITICAL | Core hypothesis with major evidential gap; requires adversarial review before release |
