# 10 — MASTER LEXICON
## Da Valenca Archive: Key Terms & Definitions

### Structural Terms
- **Corpus Leonardianum Universale** — overarching title for the full reconstruction project
- **Da Valenca** — authorship and archive family identity
- **Grammatica Naturae** — the grammar of nature; governing philosophical frame
- **Lex Motus** — law of motion; mechanical spine
- **Ars Vivens** — living art; painting as proof

### Codex Terms
- **BNE** — Biblioteca Nacional de España (holds Madrid Codices)
- **BNE numbering** — inverse: 5958 = f.0r; decreasing numbers = increasing folios
- **PC codes** — Priority Claims (PC-1 through PC-9)
- **Three-layer model** — Text/Reverse/Margins reading system
- **Seepage** — ink bleed indicating continuation of structure across folios

### Mechanical Terms
- **Central shaft** — primary power axis; alignment and transmission spine
- **Sensale** — Leonardo's term for the transmission shaft
- **Fusee** — conical spiral for equalising spring tension
- **Toggle-lock** — over-centre locking mechanism (PC-6)
- **Lunar gear cascade** — 1:29.5 ratio orrery gear train (PC-3)

### Project Terms
- **Figura Ultima** — Turin Shroud authorship thesis
- **SolarScorch** — solar geometry alignment study
- **Principia Omnium** — universal architecture of motion thesis
