## Design Principle

Each section is both:
1. A **standalone repository paper** — independently citable
2. An **integrated corpus chapter** — connected to the chronological narrative

The corpus does not duplicate paper content. Section files are navigation and framing layers only. All primary content remains in the standalone repositories.
