## Historical Context

The Figura Ultima thesis proposes that Leonardo created the Turin Shroud circa 1476–1478, during the gap years between his Florence formation and his first Milan period. The Ottoman letters (Richter §§1336–7) are treated as supporting evidence for a Leonardo presence in the Armenia/Syria region during this window — the same window as the proposed production date. This is a Tier C hypothesis under investigation.
