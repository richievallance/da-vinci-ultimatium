## SECTION A — Primary Manuscripts

### A.1 Madrid Codices (BNE)

Leonardo da Vinci. *Trattato di Statica e Meccanica* (Codex Madrid I). Biblioteca Nacional de España, Ms.8937. c.1493–1497. Digital facsimile and interactive BNE transcription viewer: https://leonardo.bne.es ✓

Leonardo da Vinci. *Codex Madrid II.* Biblioteca Nacional de España, Ms.8936. c.1503–1505. ✓

### A.2 Codex Atlanticus

Leonardo da Vinci. *Codex Atlanticus.* Biblioteca Ambrosiana, Milan. 1,119 leaves, c.1478–1519. Edited by Augusto Marinoni. 24 vols. Florence: Giunti, 1973–1980. ✓

Leonardo da Vinci. *Codex Atlanticus* (digital edition). Biblioteca Ambrosiana / Veneranda Biblioteca Ambrosiana. https://www.ambrosiana.it/en/collections/codex-atlanticus/ ✓

### A.3 Windsor Collection

Leonardo da Vinci. Royal Collection Trust, Windsor, RCIN 912281–919120 (anatomical and other drawings). https://www.rct.uk/collection/themes/exhibitions/leonardo-da-vinci-a-life-in-drawing ✓

### A.4 Forster Codices

Leonardo da Vinci. *Forster Codices I–III.* Victoria and Albert Museum, London, MSL/1876/Forster/141/I–III. Edited by Augusto Marinoni. Florence: Giunti, 1992. ✓

### A.5 Codex Leicester

Leonardo da Vinci. *Codex Leicester* (Codex Hammer). Private collection (Bill Gates). c.1506–1510. Digital edition: https://www.codexleicester.com ✓

### A.6 Codex Arundel

Leonardo da Vinci. *Codex Arundel.* British Library, London, Arundel MS 263. Edited by C. Pedretti and C. Vecce. Florence: Giunti, 1998. British Library Digitised Manuscripts: https://www.bl.uk/manuscripts/FullDisplay.aspx?ref=Arundel_MS_263 ✓

### A.7 Manuscript D (Optics)

Leonardo da Vinci. *Manuscript D.* Institut de France, Paris, Ms.D. c.1508–1509. Digital edition via BVMM (Bibliothèque virtuelle des manuscrits médiévaux): https://bvmm.irht.cnrs.fr ⚠ PENDING_VERIFICATION

### A.8 Turin Shroud

Archdiocese of Turin. *Sindone di Torino* (Turin Shroud). Cathedral of Saint John the Baptist, Turin. Carbon dating 1988: Oxford University, Arizona, ETH Zürich. Repository: Centro Internazionale di Sindonologia, Turin. ✓
