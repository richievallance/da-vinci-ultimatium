## Compression Layer Language Discipline

Every compression layer document must:

- Preserve all ECS caveats (TYPE_C and TYPE_D claims must remain qualified)
- Avoid sensationalism (no 'shocking discovery', 'secret code', 'conspiracy', 'Leonardo definitively')
- Remain falsifiable (every major claim includes falsification pathway reference)
- Keep disciplines cleanly separated (mechanica stream separate from Shroud stream)
- Include at the top: *"This material is submitted for specialist critique only. Not for public distribution. Restricted specialist review — not public validation."*
