## Chapter 1 · Biographical Framework
**Period:** 1452–1519
**Repository:** corpus-leonardianum · MASTER_CORPUS_INDEX.md

Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci was born at Anchiano, near Vinci, on 15 April 1452. His working life spans four primary phases:

| Period | Location | Codex Activity |
|---|---|---|
| 1469–1481 | Florence (Verrocchio's workshop) | Pre-codex drawings |
| 1482–1499 | Milan (Sforza court) | Madrid I active; Atlanticus begins |
| 1500–1515 | Florence, Venice, Rome | Madrid II; anatomical peak |
| 1516–1519 | Amboise, France | Late synthesis |

**Chiaravalle dating anchor (PC-4):** BNE 5892 contains an explicit reference to the Abbey of Chiaravalle, placing Madrid Codex I composition firmly within the Milan period, c.1490–1497. [PRIMARY SOURCE]
