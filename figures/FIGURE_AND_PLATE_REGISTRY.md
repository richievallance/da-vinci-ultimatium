## Governing Principles

1. Every image used in a public-facing document must have an entry in this registry.
2. Every enhanced or processed image must be labelled: **PROVISIONAL / REPLICATION REQUIRED**
3. No enhanced image may be treated as primary evidence. Enhanced images are visual aids only.
4. Every image must have a verified permission status before inclusion in peer review materials.
5. Images sourced from public domain repositories (BNE Interactivo, Windsor RCIN open access) may be used freely with attribution. Images from commercial editions require explicit permission.

---

## Permission Status Codes

- 🟢 FREE USE — Public domain or confirmed open access with attribution
- 🟡 ATTRIBUTION REQUIRED — CC licence; specific attribution terms apply
- 🔴 PERMISSION REQUIRED — Commercial or restricted use; explicit permission needed before publication
- ⚠ STATUS UNKNOWN — Permission status not confirmed; do not use in peer review materials

## Enhanced / Processed Images (All Documents)

**All enhanced imagery in this corpus is governed by this rule:**

> **PROVISIONAL / REPLICATION REQUIRED**
> This image has been digitally processed, enhanced, or composited. It functions as a visual aid only. It may not be treated as primary evidence. Any claim derived from this image requires independent verification against the original primary source.

This label must appear in the caption of every processed, enhanced, or overlaid image before peer review submission.
