## Governing Principle

Every claim in this corpus that rises to the level of an original scholarly contribution must have a clear, stated pathway by which it could be falsified. Claims that cannot be falsified are not scientific claims.

## Madrid Codex I Falsification Pathways

### PC-1: Flintlock Prototype

**Falsification condition:** Discovery of a prior published identification of BNE 5907 as a complete flintlock mechanism in any work predating this project — specifically in Reti 1974, or any subsequent publication.

**Current status:** RSV has not found such identification. Reti treats the component as part of a general weapons mechanism without identifying the complete flintlock sequence.

### PC-2: Resonant Impulse Accumulation

**Falsification condition:** (a) Discovery of a prior systematic identification of this principle as the theoretical basis of escapement physics in the Leonardo literature; OR (b) Reinterpretation of BNE 5911 that makes the bochalari passage incompatible with resonant impulse accumulation.

### PC-3: Combined Clock-and-Orrery

**Falsification condition:** (a) Demonstration that the gear cascade 12:24:4:4:59 does not produce a 1:29.5 ratio (arithmetic falsification); OR (b) Discovery of a prior unified identification of the clock and orrery as simultaneous branches of one *sensale*.

**Note:** The gear ratio arithmetic is independently verifiable. If the arithmetic is correct, this claim's core is not falsifiable on arithmetic grounds.

### PC-4: Chiaravalle Dating Anchor

**Falsification condition:** Evidence that the Chiaravalle reference in BNE 5892 refers to a different institution or a different clock; OR demonstration that the Chiaravalle clock post-dates the codex entry.

### PC-5: Seepage Alignment Methodology

**Falsification condition:** Demonstration that the observed seepage patterns are consistent with natural diffusion from adjacent leaves only, and do not require deliberate compression of non-adjacent leaves.

**This claim is the most vulnerable to falsification.** Independent examination of the physical codex is required to verify or falsify it.

### PC-6: Toggle-Lock Doctrine

**Falsification condition:** Discovery of a prior systematic identification of the over-centre toggle-lock principle in engineering literature predating this project; OR reinterpretation of BNE 5955 that makes the geometric condition description incompatible with toggle-lock analysis.

## Figura Ultima Falsification Pathways

### 7.1 Scalar Ratio

**Falsification condition:** Arithmetic demonstration that the 7.1× scalar does not produce Shroud-scale body geometry from the Vitruvian module to within acceptable measurement tolerance; OR demonstration that a range of other scalars produces equally good fits, eliminating the specificity of 7.1.

### Leonardo Authorship of the Shroud

**Falsification condition (strongest):** DNA analysis of organic material in the Shroud image layer that does not match Leonardo da Vinci's documented genetic profile (e.g., recovered from authenticated biological material at RCIN Windsor or Biblioteca Ambrosiana).

**Falsification condition (supporting):** Physical evidence of the Shroud's existence before 1476 in a form that makes Leonardo's involvement impossible on chronological grounds. (Note: current radiocarbon dating controversy means this condition cannot be invoked from the 1988 data alone.)

**Falsification condition (methodological):** Demonstration of a non-human-directed photothermal process that produces frontal and dorsal body images of this specificity without the intervention of an optical concentration device.
