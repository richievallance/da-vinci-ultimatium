## How to Use This Guide

Each pathway below is calibrated for a different specialist reader. No reviewer is expected to begin with the full corpus (~100,000 words). Begin with the pathway for your discipline, follow the sequence, and consult the full document only when a reference demands it.

All pathways begin at the same entry point: the **500-word abstract** (once generated — see compression layer). For now, begin at the recommended first document in each pathway.
