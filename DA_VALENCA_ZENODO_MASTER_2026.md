**5.2 Sub-Discovery 2.2: INK SEEPAGE MULTI-LAYER BLUEPRINT SYSTEM**

**Discovery:**

**Mechanism:**

**Process:**

3. Faint shadow appears on recto side
4. Trace shadow with black ink (permanent)
5. Result: Aligned recto/verso pair encoding same component

**Evidence:**

**Physical Evidence:**
- Sanguine faded, reworked with black ink (documented by conservators)
- Spatial optimization proven by pixel-level alignment analysis
